Why Kestrel appears and Bodnar Logistics doesn't

→ AI can't tell who Bodnar is. There's no structured entry to anchor the name, so a model confuses "Bodnar" with other businesses and can't confidently recommend it. Kestrel has that scaffolding, so every platform resolves them instantly.
→ Nothing outside Bodnar's own website points to Bodnar. AI recommendations are built from third-party sources — trade titles, directories, industry lists — and Bodnar is in none of them. Kestrel is cited across all of them, so the models learn to trust and repeat the name.
→ The result: in every query where a buyer is actually choosing a provider, Bodnar simply isn't in the room. The buyer hears a competitor's name and acts on it — and Bodnar never sees the enquiry it didn't get.
